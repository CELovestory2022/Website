Our story

I met Eziafa (my wife in waiting)😂for the first time in 2003 in our teenage years ..lol. I remember the exact path we met, what she was wearing...emmm...(I can't even remember my own out fit..shame). I remember how my heart skipped several beats and how we smiled at each other without saying a word...that for me was love at first sight..and as I kept walking I whispered to God that I would like to see her again and of cause the good lord made it happen in a few weeks. We became friends and kept it that way and we were in touch most of the time via email and yahoo messenger..lol.
In November 2012 we made our first attempt at dating which didn't last for a month ...lol. I look back now and I thank God it didn't work then...I guess we could have ruined it and lost this opportunity of forever. We remained friends even when the relationship didn't work out... I "innocently" got on her nerves many times...lol and today she calls me her former fight partner🤣. In the course of all this a few of our mutual friends and few of my own friends mentioned that we look good together. In fact one person said to me and I would state it in his words "all this one you dey do na Eziafa u go marry laslas". Some other time he would say "Chanai marry that girl na" and then there's this other one that would say " Chanai marry my friend na". In my defense I would say; she doesn't see me in that light ...lol....but here we are today by the mercies of God. He has given us a love so true, so beautiful and divine, and I know for sure that He would keep us in His love forever and a century more.
So I say to you and you: Tempus fugit, amor manet, amor vincit omnia.
Time flies, love waits, love conquers all.

Come April 30th by the grace of God we would be joined together in Holy Matrimony... So I invite you to come witness and honour us with your presence.
